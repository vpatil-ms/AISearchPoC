California has enacted legislation (Senate Bill 114) that requires employers with more than 25 employees to provide up to 80 hours of COVID-19 supplemental paid sick leave to employees. The COVID-19 supplemental paid sick leave requirement is effective on February 19, 2022, is retroactive to January 1, 2022 and will remain in effect through September 30, 2022. Employees can request the leave orally or in writing.
