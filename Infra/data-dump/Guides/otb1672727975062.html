Termination Overview

The termination process should always be handled with extreme care. This means that proper precautions should be taken before, during, and after the termination meeting.

This guide will cover the termination process as well as policy and practice considerations, including:

  • How to handle voluntary and involuntary terminations
  • Tips for conducting exit interviews
  • Guidelines for no call/no show and job abandonment policies
  • Furloughs vs layoffs
  • At-will employment
  • Notification requirements under the WARN Act
  • Providing references for terminated employees

This guide and the information available through it do not, and are not intended to, constitute legal advice. Consult legal counsel for any specific questions about termination.
