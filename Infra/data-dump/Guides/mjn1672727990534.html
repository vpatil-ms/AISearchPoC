Make Introductions and Promote Communication

Within their first few days on the job, introduce new employees to key members of the company and their team. These introductions typically only take a short amount of time but can go a long way in helping employees feel welcomed. Have a few questions ready to spark conversation and allow the team to get to know one another. Then, schedule regular meetings between new hires and their co-workers. Also, hold supervisors accountable for maintaining communication with new hires and including them in key meetings.
