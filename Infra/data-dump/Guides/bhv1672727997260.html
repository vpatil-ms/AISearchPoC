EEO for Small Businesses

To determine which laws you must follow, you need to check into three sets of laws: federal laws; the laws of the state in which you operate; and the local laws of the city and county in which you operate.

The majority of federal anti-discrimination laws limit their application to businesses with 15 or more employees. There are three exceptions to this general rule:

  • Age Discrimination in Employment Act: which applies to employers with 20 or more employees
  • Immigration Reform and Control Act: which applies to employers with one or more employees, and
  • Equal Pay Act: which applies to employers with one or more employees.
