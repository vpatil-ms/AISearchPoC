Handbook Acknowledgments

Obtaining signed handbook acknowledgments confirms that employees have read, understand, and are prepared to comply with company policies. Here are some guidelines related to the process:

Obtain initial acknowledgment:

When the handbook is first introduced, employees should review the entire document and sign an acknowledgment form. Retain signed acknowledgments in employees' personnel files.

After revisions, obtain another acknowledgment:

Anytime a revision is made to the employee handbook, inform employees of the change and that they are responsible for reviewing the revised policy. All employees should sign a new acknowledgment when there has been a change in policy or a new policy has been added to the handbook.
