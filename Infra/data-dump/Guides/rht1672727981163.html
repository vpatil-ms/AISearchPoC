The Claims Process

For employees to file a claim, they must contact the State Unemployment Insurance agency as soon as possible after being terminated. In some states, filing a claim is now possible via phone or the Internet.

Typically, there is a two to three week waiting period before the individual receives their first benefit check. Some states require a one-week waiting period and therefore assert the second week claimed as the first week of payment.

When claims are filed employees are asked questions about their residence and former employment. Employment-related questions address: the length of employment with their previous employer, the reason for leaving their previous job, and wages earned.
