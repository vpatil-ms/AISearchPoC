Compensation Overview

Compensation involves much more than a paycheck. Today's employees value total compensation. Total compensation includes both monetary and non-monetary awards, and can be thought of as the sum of all types of compensation provided to employees, including but not limited to:

  • Salary/wages
  • Benefits
  • Paid time off
  • Incentive pay
  • Bonuses
  • Commissions

Employee compensation should be strategically designed to attract, motivate, and retain talented employees.
