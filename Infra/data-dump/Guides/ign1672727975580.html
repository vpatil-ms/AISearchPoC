Workers' Compensation Overview

Workers' Compensation (WC) is an insurance program that provides disability benefits and medical coverage for workers who suffer from work-related injuries or illnesses, regardless of whether the employee or the employer was negligent.

Since workers' compensation is so circumstantial, every situation must be looked at individually with particular attention focused on specific details. Each company should consult with their lawyer, insurance carrier and state official to determine their liability and course of action. In some cases, other laws such as the FMLA or ADA might also come into play.
