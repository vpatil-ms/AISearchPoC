Employee Access to Personnel Files

While there is no federal requirement to grant employees access to their own personnel files, there are several states that do. Most of these states permit employees to view their files within a “reasonable period of time” following a written request. Check your state law to ensure compliance.

If your state does not have a requirement for employees to access their personnel records, you may want to consider adopting it as practice. Just be sure you are consistent when it comes to handling the issue. If you permit one employee to view their records, you must allow access for all employees who request it.
