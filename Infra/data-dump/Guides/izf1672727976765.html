Overview

There are several federal, state, and local laws that require employers to provide employees with job-protected leave. These laws typically address the circumstances in which employees can take leave, whether the leave is paid or unpaid, how much time employees can take, requirements for providing advance notice, and reinstatement following leave.

In this Toolkit, we provide an overview of some of the major leave laws impacting employers. This list is not inclusive of all leave requirements. For more information on the specific laws that apply to your business, see the Leave of Absence sections in the State & Federal Compliance Database.
