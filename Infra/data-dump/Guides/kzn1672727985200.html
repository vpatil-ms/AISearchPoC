Set Expectations

Schedule a meeting for the employee's first day to set expectations. During the meeting, provide your new hire with an agenda for the first week or so. Review job responsibilities, objectives, and expectations and explain how their role contributes to the success of the company. Instruct the employee to review your employee handbook and discuss key issues, such as work schedules, timekeeping practices, and how performance is measured. Obtain the employee's acknowledgment of the handbook and retain it in their personnel file.
