Discipline Overview

When an employee's conduct or performance becomes an issue, an effective disciplinary process can help you work with the employee to correct the problem and prevent it from reoccurring. This guide includes guidelines for preventing and responding to performance and conduct concerns.

Note: This guide and the information available through it do not, and are not intended to, constitute legal advice. Consult legal counsel for any specific questions about disciplinary issues.
