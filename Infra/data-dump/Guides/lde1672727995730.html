Equal Pay

The Equal Pay Act (EPA) amended the FLSA to prohibit employers from gender-based pay discrimination. The EPA requires employers to pay all employees equally for work requiring the same or substantiality similar skill, effort, and responsibility. There are a few limited exceptions, including payments made pursuant to a:

  • Seniority system
  • Merit system
  • System that measures earnings by quantity or quality of production
  • Differential based on any other factor other than sex
