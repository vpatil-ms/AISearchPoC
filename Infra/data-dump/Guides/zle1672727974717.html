Performance Management Overview

Performance management is an ongoing process that involves setting clear performance goals, providing regular feedback, coaching and developing employees, and rewarding and recognizing employee efforts.

Typically, the performance management process includes the following elements:

  • Training supervisors
  • Setting clear goals
  • Providing regular constructive feedback
  • Conducting formal performance reviews regularly
  • Linking performance to rewards
  • Providing poor performers with an opportunity to improve
  • Documenting performance-related discussion
