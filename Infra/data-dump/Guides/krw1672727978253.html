Negative Effects of Sexual Harassment

Women and men have the right to a work environment that is free of sexual harassment or unwanted demands for romantic or sexual relationships, or undesired communication or behaviors of a sexual nature that interfere with their ability to work.

There are many negative effects of sexual harassment that can be deleterious to your employees and your organization. These effects can spread to all parts of your organization beyond just the employees who were directly involved. Some of the negative effects include:

  • An unsafe work environment
  • Potentially harmful mental and emotional distress
  • Physical threats
  • Decreased productivity
  • Decreased morale
  • Feelings of a lack of respect
  • Loss of professional standards within an organization
  • Distractions from work
  • Higher employee turnover
  • Feelings of unfairness

Keep in mind that these negative effects may not only manifest themselves in the victims and harassers, but all those who are around the harassing behavior.
