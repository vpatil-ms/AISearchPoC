Overview

Employers are required to verify that new hires are authorized to work in the United States by completing the I-9 Form (Employment Eligibility Verification) for each new hire.

The federal government has also created an online electronic verification system known as “E-Verify.” Currently, there is no federal requirement for private employers to use E-Verify; however, some federal contractors are required to use it. In addition, a number of states impose E-Verify mandates. Absent a requirement, employers may use E-Verify voluntarily. However, even when E-Verify is used, employers must still complete and retain a Form I-9 for each new hire.

This guide provides information on completing and retaining I-9 Forms and using E-Verify.
