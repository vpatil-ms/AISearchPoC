What is an Employee Handbook?

An employee handbook is a valuable tool for communicating information to employees. Whether written or not, most companies already have policies and procedures governing their employment practices. Creating an employee handbook formalizes those policies so that all employees are familiar with company expectations.

An employee handbook:

  • Describes appropriate and inappropriate workplace conduct
  • Informs employees of their at-will status (i.e., generally, absent certain exceptions, both the employee and employer may terminate the employment relationship at any time, for any reason)
  • Provides employees with an overview of company benefits
  • Helps to orient new employees to the company
  • Helps to ensure all employees are held to the same standards and that all supervisors follow set procedures
  • Meets certain requirements that specific employment-related information be provided to employees in writing
