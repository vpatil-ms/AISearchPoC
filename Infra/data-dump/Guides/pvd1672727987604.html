Executive Order 13665: Pay Transparency Nondiscrimination

Executive Order 13665 (also known as the Pay Transparency Nondiscrimination Provision) prohibits federal contractors and subcontractors from discharging, or otherwise discriminating against, employees or applicants who inquire about, discuss, or disclose their compensation or the compensation of other employees or applicants. An exception exists where the employee or applicant makes the disclosure based on information obtained in the course of performing his or her essential job functions. Executive Order 13665 applies to any contractor or subcontractor covered by Executive Order 11246.

Covered employers must post the Pay Transparency Nondiscrimination Provision in the workplace and include it in employee manuals and handbooks. The posting requirement may be satisfied by posting the provision electronically or by posting a copy of the provision in conspicuous places available for employees and applicants.
