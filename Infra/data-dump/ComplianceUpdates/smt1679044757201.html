Vermont has announced that the state minimum wage will increase to $13.18 per hour, and the cash minimum wage rate for tipped workers will increase to $6.59 per hour on January 1, 2023.

Next Steps:

Beginning on January 1, 2023, Vermont employers should pay all non-exempt employees at least the minimum wage per hour. Employers also should post the updated minimum wage notice, once it is made available by the state, in the workplace.
