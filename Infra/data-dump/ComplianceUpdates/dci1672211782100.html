New York has enacted legislation (Assembly Bill 5240-A) that requires employers to designate single-occupancy restrooms as gender-neutral. Assembly Bill 5240-A takes effect on March 23, 2021.

Assembly Bill 5240-A:

Assembly Bill 5240-A requires that all single-occupancy bathroom facilities located in public places (including but not limited to those located in restaurants, bars, mercantile establishments, or factories) must be designated as gender neutral for use by no more than one occupant at a time or for family or assisted use. This designation should be clearly displayed on or near the entry door of each gender neutral bathroom facility.

Compliance Recommendations:

Covered employers should review their policies and practices to ensure compliance with Assembly Bill 5240-A.
