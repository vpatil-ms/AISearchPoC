Missouri's minimum wage will increase to $12.00 per hour on January 1, 2023. Tipped employees must be paid at least $6.00 per hour (half of the state minimum wage).

Note: Employers engaged in retail or service businesses whose annual gross income is less than $500,000 are not required to pay the state minimum wage rate.

Next Steps:

Missouri employers should ensure compliance with the minimum wage requirements, and once published by the state, post the updated minimum wage poster in a conspicuous location.
