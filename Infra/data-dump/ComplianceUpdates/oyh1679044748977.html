The City of Milpitas, California has announced that the minimum wage for the city will increase.

The Details:

Effective July 1, 2022, the minimum wage will increase from $15.65 per hour to $16.40 per hour.

Note: California does not allow the use of a tip credit when paying tipped employees.

Next Steps:

Employers in Milpitas, California should as of July 1, 2022, pay their employees at least $16.40 per hour. In addition, employers must post a notice in the workplace advising employees of the increased minimum wage. This notice may be found here once provided.
