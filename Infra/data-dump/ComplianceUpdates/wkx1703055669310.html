Washington state has announced its 2024 Paid Family & Medical Leave (PFML) premiums and has released the 2024 taxable wage bases and rates when calculating the state's PFML premiums.

The Details

2024 PFML Premiums

The total PFML premium will be 0.74% for 2024. Employers with 50 or more employees will be responsible for 28.57% of the total premium and employees will be responsible for 71.43%.

Businesses having fewer than 50 employees are not required to pay the employer portion of the premium, unless they choose to do so. However, they must still collect the employee premium or pay employees' premiums on their behalf.

Next Steps

Effective January 1, 2024, covered Washington state employers must pay the required premium, collect premiums from employees via wage deductions and remit to the Washington State Department of Paid Family & Medical Leave.
