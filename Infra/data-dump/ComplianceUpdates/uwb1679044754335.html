Colorado has announced that the state's minimum wage will increase.

The Details:

Effective January 1, 2023:

  • The state minimum wage will increase from $12.56 per hour to $13.65 per hour.
  • Tipped employees (workers who customarily receive more than $30 per month in tips) must receive at least $10.63 per hour in cash wages.

Next Steps:

As of the first day of 2023, Colorado employers must pay employees at least the minimum wage as outlined above.
