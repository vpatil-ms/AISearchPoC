Berkeley, California has announced that its minimum wage will increase.

The Details:

Effective July 1, 2022, the minimum wage will increase to $16.99 per hour for all employees including tipped employees. The current minimum wage rate is $16.32 per hour.

Note: California does not allow the use of a tip credit when paying tipped employees.

Next Steps:

On July 1, 2022, employers in Berkeley must pay employees who perform work in the city at least $16.99 per hour. In addition, employers must post “where employees can easily read” the minimum wage notice found here.
