The Virginia General Assembly has delayed a scheduled increase to the minimum wage.

Background:

Assembly Bill 395 increases Virginia's minimum wage in phases. The first scheduled increase was set to take place on January 1, 2021. However, the General Assembly has announced a delay to allow employers more time to recover from COVID-19.

Updated Minimum Wage Schedule:

Compliance Recommendations:

Beginning May 1, 2021, Virginia employers must pay all non-exempt employees at least $9.50 per hour. Virginia employers must also post an updated minimum wage notice in a conspicuous area of the workplace. Once published by the state, the 2021 minimum wage notice will be available for download from the State & Federal Resources section of HR411®.
