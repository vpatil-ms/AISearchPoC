The Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2023 (HR 2617) was recently signed into law. The Act includes provisions affecting retirement savings plans. These provisions, collectively referred to as SECURE 2.0, offer many new benefits to employers and employees that are designed to make it more attractive for employers to offer retirement plans and to improve retirement outcomes for employees.

SECURE 2.0 contains some 90 changes to retirement savings plans which should be reviewed in depth for applicability. Some provisions may require amendments to existing retirement plans and offerings. Employers should consult with appropriate legal counsel and financial professionals to identify any changes that must be made and determine what optional provisions may be beneficial.
