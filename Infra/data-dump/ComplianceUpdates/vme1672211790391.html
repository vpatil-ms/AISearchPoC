Oregon has enacted legislation (House Bill 3041) that amends the Oregon Equality Act to define gender identity. House Bill 3041 is effective immediately.

Overview:

The Oregon Equality Act prohibits employers from discriminating against applicants and employees on the basis of sexual orientation and gender identity.

House Bill 3041:

House Bill 3041 amends the Oregon Equality Act to provide a definition of gender identity. Under the law, gender identity is an individual's gender-related identity, appearance, expression or behavior, regardless of whether the identity, appearance, expression or behavior differs from that associated with the gender assigned to the individual at birth.

Compliance Recommendation:

Oregon employers should review their policies and procedures to ensure compliance with House Bill 3041.
