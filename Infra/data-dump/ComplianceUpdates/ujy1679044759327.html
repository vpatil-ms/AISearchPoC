Virginia's minimum wage will increase to $12.00 per hour on January 1, 2023.

The Details:

Virginia's minimum wage is scheduled to increase as follows:

Date

Hourly Minimum Wage Rate

January 1, 2023

$12.00

January 1, 2025

$13.50 *

January 1, 2026

$15.00 *

*The January 1, 2025, and January 1, 2026 minimum wage changes must be enacted by the General Assembly by July 1, 2024 to take effect.

House Bill 395:

Additionally, under House Bill 395, employers are:

  • Allowed to pay employees that are enrolled in an on-the-job training program no less than the federal minimum wage or 75 percent of Virginia's minimum wage for up to 90 days; and
  • Required to pay at least the minimum wage to individuals who:
    • Are employed by businesses with less than four employees;
    • Are employed in domestic service or a private home, such as nannies;
    • Normally work and are paid based on the amount of work done or completed; or
    • Have impaired earning capacities due to physical deficiency, mental illness, or intellectual disability.

Next Steps:

  • Comply with the new minimum wage by January 1, 2023.
  • Ensure that workers (including those added under House Bill 395) are paid the required hourly minimum wage.
