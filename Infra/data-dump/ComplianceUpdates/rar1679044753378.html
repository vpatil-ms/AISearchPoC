Denver Minimum Wage Set to Increase January 1, 2023

The City of Denver has announced that its minimum wage will increase from $15.87 per hour to $17.29 per hour.

The Details:

Effective January 1, 2023, the Denver minimum wage will be $17.29 per hour. On this same date, tipped employees must be paid cash wages in the amount of $14.27 per hour (currently $12.85 per hour).

Next Steps:

As of January 1, 2023, Denver employers must pay its non-tipped employees at least $17.29 per hour. Tipped employees must be paid at least $14.27 per hour in cash wages.
