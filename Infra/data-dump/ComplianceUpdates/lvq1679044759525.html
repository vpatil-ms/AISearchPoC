The Indiana Department of Revenue (DOR) has issued a revised version of Departmental Notice #1 , which includes a change to their state withholding calculation effective October 1, 2022. To accommodate the change to the withholding calculation, the DOR has revised the Indiana “Employee's Withholding Exemption and County Status Certificate” commonly known as Form WH-4.

The Details:

The calculation includes a new additional annual exemption amount for adopted qualifying dependents that can now be claimed on an employee's Form WH-4. Most employees are entitled to deduct an additional $3,000 per year per qualifying adopted dependent claimed on line 7 of their Form WH-4.
