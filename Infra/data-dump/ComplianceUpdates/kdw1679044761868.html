Mountain View, California has adopted a Wage Theft Ordinance.

The Details

The Ordinance, effective January 1, 2023, states that all employers required to have a City of Mountain View business license must complete a “Wage Theft Affidavit” when applying for a city business license and when submitting business license renewals to the tax administrator.

In the affidavit, the employer must affirm either that they:

  1. Have not been found in violation of a federal, state, or local wage-and-hour law; or
  2. Are in compliance with or have fully satisfied any final judgment, order, or administrative decision issued against them for violating applicable federal, state, or local wage-and-hour laws.

Next Steps

All employers required to have a Mountain View business license should complete the required affidavit when they apply to or renew their city business license.

Mountain View has provided a model Wage Theft Affidavit for use in fulfilling the requirement.
