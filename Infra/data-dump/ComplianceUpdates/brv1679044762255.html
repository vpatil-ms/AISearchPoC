The New York Department of Labor has enacted legislation (Senate Bill 1961B), which requires employers to display a poster on veterans' benefits and services. Senate Bill 1961B is effective immediately.

The Details

Under the law, New York employers with more than 50 full-time equivalent employees must display the required poster on benefits and services available to military veterans in a conspicuous place that is accessible to employees in the workplace.

Next Steps

New York employers should display the required poster to help ensure compliance with Senate Bill 1961B.
