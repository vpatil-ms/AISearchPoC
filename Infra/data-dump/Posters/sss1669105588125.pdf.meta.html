IA-fair housing poster (spanish)

Who must post

Required for all IA employers involved in real estate or tenancy.

Where to obtain

Iowa Civil Rights Commission: http://www.state.ia.us/government/crc/publications/posters.html

Law relates to

Civil Rights Law; Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO)laws

Source URL

http://www.state.ia.us/government/crc/publications/posters.html

Misc. info

This is the Spanish version of the poster explaining Iowa citizens' rights in terms of equal opportunity in the terms and conditions of buying, renting, or borrowing.
