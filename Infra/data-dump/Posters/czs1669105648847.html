Employers with five or more employees for a 20-week period in the current or preceding year must provide reasonable accommodations for pregnancy, childbirth or related medical conditions, including lactation, unless the accommodation would impose an undue hardship.

Who must post

Employers with five or more employees for a 20-week period in the current or preceding year

Where to obtain

The Virginia Division of Human Rights

Law relates to

nondiscrimination, pregnancy
