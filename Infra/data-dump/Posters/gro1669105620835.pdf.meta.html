Required for all Wyoming employers

Wyoming Department of Employment: http://wydoe.state.wy.us/search.asp?criteria=posters&submit1=Search

Occupational Safety and Health

Misc. info

This poster covers employer and employee responsibilities under the state's occupational safety and health program. Procedures for inspections, complaints, violations, and penalties are explained.
