ME-equal pay poster

Who must post

Recommended of all Maine employers

Where to obtain

Maine Department of Labor: http://www.maine.gov/labor/posters/

Law relates to

Equal Pay Act

Misc. info

This poster details Maine's Equal Pay Law, which requires that employees be paid the same wages as employees of the opposite sex for work that is of a comparable nature in skill, effort and responsibility. Equal Pay Complaint forms are also provided.
