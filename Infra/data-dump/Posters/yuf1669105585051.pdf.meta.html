Co-anti discrimination poster

Who must post

Required for all CO employers.

Where to obtain

Colorado Department of Labor and Employment: http://www.coworkforce.com/lab/postingrequirements.pdf

Law relates to

Civil Rights Law; Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO)laws

Misc. info

This is the Colorado anti-discrimination poster, which is intended to inform employees that their employer complies with Colorado anti-discrimination regulations on the basis of employment, housing, and public accommodation. For further information contact the Colorado Civil Rights Division; Address: 1560 Broadway, Suite 1050, Denver, CO 80202-5143; Phone: (303) 894-7830
