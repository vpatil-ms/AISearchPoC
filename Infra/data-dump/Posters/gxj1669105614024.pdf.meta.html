North Carolina's Labor Laws Poster (Spanish)

Who must post

Required for all North Carolina employers.

Where to obtain

NC Department of Labor: Call 1-800-625-2267, visit labor.nc.gov/workplace-rights/employer-responsibilities/all-state-and-federal-workplace-posters, or fill out the online order form here: http://www.nclabor.com/posters/posters.htm

Law relates to

North Carolina's Labor Laws

Source URL

https://www.labor.nc.gov/media/1182/open

Misc. info

This is the Spanish version of the North Carolina combined labor laws poster, including safety and health regulations, wage and hour regulations, and employment discrimination.The official poster can be obtained by contacting the North Carolina Department of Labor.
