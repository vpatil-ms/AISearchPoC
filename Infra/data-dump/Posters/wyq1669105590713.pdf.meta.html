KS-child labor poster

Who must post

Required for all KS employers that employ minors under the age of 18.

Where to obtain

Kansas Department of Labor: http://www.dol.ks.gov/es/html/posters_DBR.html

Law relates to

Fair Labor Standards Act - Child Labor

Misc. info

This poster details hours of work allowed for minors under the age of 18 in Kansas.
