This poster details employees' rights regarding Rhode Island's Sick and Safe Leave Law.

Who must post

Required for virtually all employers with employees working in RI.

Where to obtain

Rhode Island Department of Labor and Training

Law relates to

Sick Leave

Source URL

https://dlt.ri.gov/documents/pdf/ls/HSFWfactsheet.pdf

Misc. info

Employers that impose a waiting period of up to 90 days for paid sick and safe leave use by new employees must notify new employees in writing of this requirement upon hire.
