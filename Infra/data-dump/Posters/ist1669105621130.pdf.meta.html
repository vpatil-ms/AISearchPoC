This is the KS Fair Housing Poster.

Who must post

Required for all Kansas employers involved in real estate or tenancy.

Where to obtain

Kansas Human Rights Commission: http://www.khrc.net/publication.html

Law relates to

Title VII of the Civil Rights Act of 1964.

Source URL

http://www.khrc.net/publication.html

Misc. info

This poster explains Kansas regulations in regards to equal opportunity in housing without regard to race, religion, color, sex, race by association, national origin, familial status, or disabilities.
