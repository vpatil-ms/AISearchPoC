Businesses with 20+ Employees (and non-profits with 50+ Employees) Must Post This Notice.

Who must post

San Francisco businesses with 20 or more employees (and nonprofits with 50 or more employees)

Where to obtain

http://sfgsa.org/index.aspx?page=418

Law relates to

San Francisco Health Care Security Ordinance

Misc. info

San Francisco's Health Care Security Ordinance requires covered businesses to spend a minimum amount on health care benefits for each of their covered employees.
