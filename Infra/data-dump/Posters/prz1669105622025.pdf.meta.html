Min. wage poster for agricultural employees

Who must post

Required for all U.S. employers who employ agricultural workers.

Where to obtain

US Department of Labor: http://www.dol.gov/esa/regs/compliance/posters/pdf/wh1386Agrcltr.pdf

Law relates to

Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA)

Misc. info

This poster details the federal minimum wage and child labor provisions as they relate to agricultural employees.
