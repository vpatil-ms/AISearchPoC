FL - interpretive services poster

Who must post

Required in Agency programs and for recipients receiving federal financial assistance from or through the Agency of Workforce Innovation.

Where to obtain

Florida Workforce Innovation

Law relates to

The Americans with Disabilities Act

Misc. info

Informs employees of their right to interpretive and translation services at no change if they do not speak English or are deaf, hard of hearing, or sight impaired. Print on 24x18in paper
