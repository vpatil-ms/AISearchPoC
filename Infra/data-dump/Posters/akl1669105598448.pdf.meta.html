Federal Job Safety & Health Protection Poster

Who must post

Required for all employers

Where to obtain

US Department of Labor

Law relates to

Occupational Safety and Health Act

Misc. info

This poster details federal requirements under the Occupational Safety and Health (OSH) Act of 1970. Specifically, this poster informs employees of their right to notify OSHA of workplace hazards and to request an OSHA inspection. Employer obligations under the OSH Act are also listed.
