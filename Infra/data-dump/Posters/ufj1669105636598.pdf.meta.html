Covered employers must provide this notice to new employees at the time of hire.

Who must post

All employers with one or more employees who are hired to work more than 80 hours in a calendar year in New York City.

Where to obtain

NYC Department of Consumer Affairs

Law relates to

Safe and Sick Leave
