CA No Smoking Poster

Who must post

Required for all CA employers. No downloadable version of this poster is available.

Where to obtain

CA Department of Industrial Relations: http://www.dir.ca.gov/wp.asp

Law relates to

CA Labor Code section 6404.5(c)(1)

Misc. info

Employers are required to post a sign designating where smoking is prohibited/permitted in workplace. This law is enforced by local law enforcement agencies.
