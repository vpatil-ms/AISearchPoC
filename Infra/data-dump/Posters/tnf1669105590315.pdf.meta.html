IN-fair employment poster

Who must post

Required for all Indiana employers with six or more employees.

Where to obtain

Indiana Department of Workforce Development: http://www.in.gov/dwd/required_posters.html

Law relates to

Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) laws

Misc. info

This poster details Indiana Equal Employment Opportunity regulations. Unlawful discriminatory practices, such as denying equal benefits or discriminating in hiring, firing, training, etc. against employees of a protected class is also explained. Furthermore, the State of Indiana Civil Rights Commission contact information is provided for those who feel they have been victims of discrimination.
