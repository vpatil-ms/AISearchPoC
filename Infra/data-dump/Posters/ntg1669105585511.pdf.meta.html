CT-mercantile and retail for minors poster

Who must post

Required for all CT mercantile and retail employers who employ minors under the age of 18.

Where to obtain

CT Department of Labor: Wage and Workplace Standards Unit: http://www.ctdol.state.ct.us/wgwkstnd/posters.htm

Law relates to

Fair Labor Standards Act - Child Labor

Misc. info

This poster details the time and hour restrictions for employees under the age of 18 in the Mercantile industry.
