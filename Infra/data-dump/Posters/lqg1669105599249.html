CT Unemployment Insurance poster

Who must post

Required for all CT employers. No downloadable version of this poster is available. Click the link to find out more information on how to obtain this poster.

Where to obtain

CT Department of Labor: Employers Status Unit; Phone: (860) 263-6550

Law relates to

Unemployment Compensation Insurance; Section 31-222-10 of the Regulations of CT State Agencies.

Source URL

http://www.ctdol.state.ct.us/gendocs/Labor_Posters.htm

Misc. info

This poster informs CT employees that their employer is covered under unemployment compensation insurance.
