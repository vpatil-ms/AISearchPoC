Employers must notify their employees of the EITC no later than March 1 of each year. While this poster is required by the Texas Workforce Commission, pursuant to Tex. Lab. Code § 104.002(a), employers are also required to notify employees individually about the EITC (see the forms section for a sample notice).

Required for all Texas employers
