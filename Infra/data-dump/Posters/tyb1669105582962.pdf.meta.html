Arkansas employers are required to post this notice to inform employees of state regulations regarding worker's compensation under the Occupational Safety and Health Act.

Who must post

Required for all Arkansas employers

Where to obtain

AR Workers' Compensation Commission: P.O. Box 950, Little Rock, AR 72203; Phone: 501-682-3930; Website: www.awcc.state.ar.us

Law relates to

Workers' Compensation
