CA's summary of workplace injuries and illness log

Who must post

Required by all CA employers

Where to obtain

CA Department of Industrial Relations

Law relates to

Title 8; CA Code of Regulations - Occupational Safety and Health Act (OSHA)

Source URL

http://www.dir.ca.gov/wp.asp

Misc. info

Annually, from February 1 to April 30, CA employers are required to post this log summarizing work-related injuries and illnesses; however, all injuries and illnesses are to be recorded throughout the year.
