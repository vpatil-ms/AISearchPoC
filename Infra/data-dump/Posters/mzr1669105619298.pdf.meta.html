GA-employer vacation time poster (spanish)

Who must post

Required of all GA employers.

Where to obtain

Georgia Department of Labor: http://www.dol.state.ga.us/em/required_posters.htm

Law relates to

Unemployment Compensation Insurance

Misc. info

This is the Spanish version of the poster intended to inform Georgia employees that unemployment insurance is not payable when employees are on vacation, or a leave of absence at their own request.
