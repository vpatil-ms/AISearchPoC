This is Florida's Choking Poster.

Who must post

Required for all Florida restaurants and hotels. No downloadable version of this poster is available. Click the link to find out more information on how to obtain this poster.

Where to obtain

Must be obtained from the Hemlich Institute using the order form found here: http://www.heimlichinstitute.org/media/posterform.pdf

Law relates to

Occupational Safety and Health Act (OSHA)

Source URL

Heimlich Institute: http://www.heimlichinstitute.org/posters.html

Misc. info

This poster details the instructions on and emergency precautions that must be taken when someone is choking.
