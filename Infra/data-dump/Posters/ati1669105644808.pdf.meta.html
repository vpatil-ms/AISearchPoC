Employers must include in an employee handbook, or conspicuously post at its place of business, written notice concerning an employee's rights to reasonable accommodations for pregnancy, childbirth, breastfeeding, or related conditions.

Who must post

Required for all UT employers.

Where to obtain

UT Labor Commission

Law relates to

Utah Antidiscrimination Act

Source URL

https://laborcommission.utah.gov/divisions/UOSH/RequiredPosters.html

Misc. info

This poster covers employees' rights to reasonable accommodations for pregnancy, childbirth, breastfeeding, or related conditions.
