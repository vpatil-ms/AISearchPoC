CT Safety & Health Protection on the Job Poster

Who must post

Required for all CT state and local government agencies. No downloadable version of this poster is available. Click the link for more information on how to obtain this poster.

Where to obtain

Must be obtained from CT Department of Labor - Occupational Safety & Health Division (Conn-OSHA); Phone: (860) 263-6900; Address: 38 Wolcott Hill Rd., Wethersfield, CT 06109

Law relates to

Occupational Safety and Health Act (OSHA)

Source URL

Connecticut Department of Labor; Wage and Workplace Standards Unit: http://www.ctdol.state.ct.us/Labor_Posters.htm

Misc. info

This poster details CT local and state government employees' rights to a safe and healthful work environment. All other CT employers must adhere to Federal OSHA posting requirements.
