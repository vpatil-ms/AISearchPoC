Who must post

California employers with employees in household occupations not covered under an industry wage order

Where to obtain

This notice may be obtained from the Department of Industrial Relations using the following web address: http://www.dir.ca.gov/iwc/wageorderindustries.htm

Law relates to

Wages, hours and working conditions of household occupations industry

Misc. info

An occupational wage order only applies when a business is not covered by an industry wage order
