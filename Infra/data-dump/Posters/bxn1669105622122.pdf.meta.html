Minimum wage poster for: Employee Rights for Workers with Disabilities/Special Minimum Wage

Who must post

Required for all U.S. employers who employ workers with disabilities paid at commensurate wage rates

Where to obtain

U.S. Department of Labor: http://www.dol.gov/esa/regs/compliance/posters/pdf/disabc.pdf

Law relates to

Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA); Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)

Misc. info

This poster details minimum wage provisions for disabled employees paid at commensurate wage rates. Such wages are based on the worker's individual productivity, no matter how limited, in proportion to the wage and productivity of experienced workers who do not have disabilities that impact their productivity when performing essential the same type, quality, and quantity of work.
