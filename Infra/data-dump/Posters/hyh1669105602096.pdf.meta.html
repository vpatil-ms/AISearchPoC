Alabama's workers' compensation poster

Who must post

Required for all Alabama employers.

Where to obtain

Alabama's Department of Industrial Relations: http://www.alabamainteractive.org/alabamainteractive_shell/Welcome.do?url=http://www.alabamainteractive.org/alabamainteractive_shell/Welcome.do?url=http://dir.alabama.gov/docs/doc_type.aspx?id=2

Law relates to

Workers' Compensation

Misc. info

This poster provides Alabama employees with information in regards to workers' compensation. The employer's workers' compensation insurance carrier is listed as well as their contact information.
