South Carolina's Workers' Compensation Poster

Who must post

Required for all South Carolina employers

Where to obtain

SC Department of Labor: http://www.llr.state.sc.us/aboutus/index.asp?file=DOPI.HTM#poster

Law relates to

Workers' Compensation

Misc. info

This poster informs employees of the necessary procedures to take if injured on the job. Workers' compensation pay is also referenced.
