The sick leave ordinance requires every covered employer to post in a conspicuous place at each place of business where any covered employee works within the geographic boundaries of Cook County a notice advising employees of their rights. Employers must also provide employees with a notice at the date of coverage or date of eligibility (whichever is later) and annually thereafter.

Who must post

All employers covered by the Cook County sick leave ordinance
