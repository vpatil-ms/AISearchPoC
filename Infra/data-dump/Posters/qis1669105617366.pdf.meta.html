ME-sexual harassment poster

Who must post

Required of all Maine employers

Where to obtain

Maine Department of Labor: http://www.maine.gov/labor/posters/

Law relates to

Maine Human Rights Act; Sexual Harassment

Misc. info

This poster explains unlawful conduct in regards to sexual harassment, including: unwelcome sexual advances, suggestive or lewd remarks, unwanted touches, requests for sexual favors, and retaliation.
