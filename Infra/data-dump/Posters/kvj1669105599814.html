KY- Worker's Compensation Insurance Poster

Who must post

Required for all KY employers. Click the link to find out more information on how to obtain this poster.

Where to obtain

Kentucky statute requires every employer to display a Workers' Compensation Posting Notice. The workers' compensation insurance carrier should provide this notice at each policy renewal.

Law relates to

Workers' Compensation Insurance

Source URL

Kentucky Department of Labor; Enforcement: http://www.labor.ky.gov/workersclaims/coveragecompliance/enforcement/

Misc. info

This posting must provide current policy information and instructions to employees for reporting workplace injuries. Contact your insurance agent or workers' compensation carrier for a current notice if you did not receive one with your latest policy.
