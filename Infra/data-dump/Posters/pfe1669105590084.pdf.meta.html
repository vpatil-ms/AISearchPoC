Employers of minors who are 14, 15, 16 or 17 years of age are required by law to post the maximum number of hours that minors may be permitted to work in each day of the week. The information must be posted in a conspicuous place.

Who must post

Required for all Indiana employers

Where to obtain

Indiana Department of Workforce Development: http://www.in.gov/dwd/required_posters.html

Law relates to

Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA) - Child Labor

Misc. info

This posters details Indiana regulations in regards to the employment of minors. Work hour restrictions and break requirements are explained.
