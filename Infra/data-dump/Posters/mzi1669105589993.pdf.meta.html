Employers are required to post information regarding unemployment insurance. Employers must also provide this information to employees at the time of separation from employment.

Who must post

Required for all Indiana employers. Must also be provided at the time of separation from employment.

Where to obtain

Indiana Department of Workforce Development: http://www.in.gov/dwd/required_posters.html

Law relates to

Unemployment compensation

Misc. info
