CA employers using industrial trucks must post and enforce a set of operating rules.

Who must post

Required for all CA employers operating forklifts and other types of industrial trucks or tow tractors.

Where to obtain

CA Department of Industrial Relations: http://www.dir.ca.gov/wp.asp

Law relates to

Job Safety & Health

Misc. info

This poster details CA employer regulations for operating forklifts and other types of industrial trucks or tow tractors.
