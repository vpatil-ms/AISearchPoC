DC Child Labor Law Poster

Who must post

Required for all DC employers who employ minors under 18. Click the link to find out more information on how to obtain this poster.

Where to obtain

Must be obtained from the DC Office of Wage-Hour; Phone: (202) 671-1880

Law relates to

Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA) - Child Labor

Misc. info

This poster details DC regulations regarding the employment of minors.
