This is the Ohio unemployment insurance compensation poster.

Who must post

Required for all Ohio Employers

Where to obtain

Ohio Department of Job and Family Services: http://das.ohio.gov/hrd/laborlawposters.html

Law relates to

Unemployment Compensation

Source URL

http://das.ohio.gov/hrd/laborlawposters.html

Misc. info

This is the Ohio unemployment compensation notice, which informs Ohio employees that their employer provides unemployment compensation coverage. Contact information for eligible employees to apply for unemployment compensation benefits is also provided.
