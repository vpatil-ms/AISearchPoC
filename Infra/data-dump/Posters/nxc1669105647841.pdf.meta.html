Texas employers liable under the Texas Unemployment Compensation Act and subject to the Texas Payday Law, must display this poster.

Who must post

Businesses whose employees are entitled to file for state unemployment benefits and are also covered by the Texas Payday Law

Where to obtain

.statussection@twc.state.tx.us

Law relates to

Unemployment & Payday
