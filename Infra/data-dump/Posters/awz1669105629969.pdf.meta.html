This poster can be used to satisfy notice obligations under the state's paid sick leave law, which requires employers with 50 or more employees to provide paid sick leave to service employees.

Who must post

Required for CT employers with 50 or more employees.

Where to obtain

Connecticut Department of Labor

Law relates to

Paid Sick Leave

Misc. info

Under the law, employers with 50 or more employees must provide paid sick leave to service workers.
