Provides information about rights of California employees working with hazardous/toxic substances

Who must post

Required for all CA employers that employ Spanish speaking workers and use hazardous/toxic substances.

Where to obtain

CA Department of Industrial Relations: http://www.dir.ca.gov/wp.asp

Law relates to

Title 8; CA Code of Regulations; section 3204

Misc. info

This poster details CA employees' rights to see and copy medical and exposure records.
