Employers where minors under 19 years of age are working are required to display this poster (must also show maximum number of hours persons under 19 years of age may work on each day of the week).

Who must post

Required for all AL employers who employ minors under the age of 19.

Where to obtain

Alabama Department of Industrial Relations

Law relates to

Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA) - Child Labor

Source URL

https://www.labor.alabama.gov/docs/doc_type.aspx?id=2

Misc. info

Must also show maximum number of hours persons under 19 years of age may work on each day of the week.
